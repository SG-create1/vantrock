06B · Allied Capability

Residential plotting —
an allied opportunity, not a pivot.

A disciplined, opportunistic residential plotting capability exists inside the platform for one reason: it sharpens our land-acquisition economics. Vantrock is not a residential developer. We are an industrial and real-assets platform that occasionally, where the parcel demands it, monetises an allied residential tranche on the same holding.

Allied · Opportunistic · Scale-enabling

Why the platform touches residential plotting at all.

Large industrial and logistics land parcels in India frequently carry a mixed-use character. A portion is zoned or contiguous to residential catchments. A disciplined allied residential plotting capability converts that reality into a structural advantage — not a mandate creep.

The capability lets us acquire meaningfully larger land holdings than a pure-play industrial developer can underwrite, hold them longer at lower effective blended cost, and recycle the non-industrial portion as residential plotted inventory. The core business remains industrial. The residential play is the mechanism.

An allied capability deployed to acquire industrial land at scale — never a standalone residential business.

— Why it matters
  • 01Scale. Lets us acquire multi-hundred-acre parcels that pure-play industrial developers cannot underwrite profitably on their own.
  • 02Blended cost. The residential portion monetises on its own cadence; the effective per-acre cost of the retained industrial land falls materially.
  • 03JV optionality. The capability opens joint venture and joint development structures with landowners we would otherwise walk past.
  • 04Ecosystem value. Tenant workforce catchments, ancillary infrastructure, and township-adjacent logistics all benefit from residential proximity.
  • 05Capital velocity. Residential plotted sales recycle on a disciplined cadence, feeding the platform's compounding engine.
— What this is not
Vantrock is not a residential real estate developer. Residential plotting is deployed selectively, as an allied land-acquisition instrument, inside an industrial and real-assets platform.
